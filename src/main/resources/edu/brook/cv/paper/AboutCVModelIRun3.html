Civil Violence Model
See Modeling Civil Violence: An Agent-Based Computational Approach
Joshua M. Epstein, John D. Steinbruner, and Miles T. Parker
CSED Working Paper #20
January 2001

There are two variants of the Civil Violence model. In the first, a central authority seeks to suppress decentralized rebellion. In the second, a central authority seeks to suppress communal violence between two warring ethnic groups.
